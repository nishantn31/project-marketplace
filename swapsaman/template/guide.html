How It Works

Fast, easy and free to post an Ad and you will find, what you are looking for.

Sell your product

1. Post a new Ad for the product you want to sell.

2. Fill in the details about your product and set a selling price.

3. Get price quoted by the buyers and their details on your posted Ad in "My Ads" section.

4. Finalise the deal by negotiating with the buyer and make transaction with any mode of your preference.

Buy a product

1. Browse for the product you want to buy.

2. Fill in the details in the form on the product page to quote your price to the seller directly.

3. Contact the seller directly to start negotiation or wait for their response.

4. Finalise the deal with seller at your requested price and make transaction to buy the product.
